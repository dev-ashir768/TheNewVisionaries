Vision

The vision of an organisation serves as a guiding light, illuminating the path toward its desired future state. Our vision is centered on a commitment to excellence, innovation, and impact. We envision a country where our services empower individuals and communities, driving positive change and sustainable growth.

Through continuous learning, collaboration, and a relentless pursuit of excellence, we aim to be pioneers in our community, shaping the future and making a lasting difference in the lives of people.

Mission

The New Visionaries (TNV) is a non-profit organisation founded by young students in February 2021. Since its inception, TNV has grown into an organisation that provides a forum for all enthusiastic learners of global affairs. Our goal is to empower students by developing their skills and helping them reach their full potential guided by the vision of An organisation of the students, by the students, for the students.

Model International Court of Justice 2023

Read more
Date:  ;November 10-12, 2023

Justice, Advocacy, Representation!

TNVMUN Edition II, held in November 2023, featured a Model International Court of Justice (Model ICJ) committee organised by the Chapter. The committee included over 25 delegates representing nations worldwide, who participated as either Applicant or Respondent in the case of “Ukraine v. The Russian Federation.”

The committee was chaired by Mr. Haziq Ali Khan as President and Mr. Ayaan Sheryar as Vice President. The Model ICJ committee provided a platform for law students to engage in a simulated court proceeding to showcase their knowledge and skills in international law and advocacy.

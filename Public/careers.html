Join Us

Avenues to Explore in the Chapter:

As a member of this Chapter, you will have access to a range of exciting events and opportunities, including:

Model United Nations (MUNs): Participate in simulated UN conferences to discuss and debate global issues.

Mock Trials: Engage in legal simulations to enhance your understanding of the judicial system.

Model PNA: Experience parliamentary debates and discussions to develop critical thinking and communication skills.

Webinars: Attend informative webinars conducted by experts in various fields, covering topics such as current affairs, leadership, and legal studies.

Workshops: Enhance your understanding of legal procedures and advocacy techniques through hands-on workshops tailored for mock trials and law-related activities.

Why Join the Chapter?

The Chapter is a student-oriented organisation dedicated to unlocking the full potential of young individuals like you. By joining the Chapter, you will have the opportunity to:

Unlock Your Potential: Engage with like-minded peers and unlock your core potential.

Community Engagement: Make a meaningful impact on your community through various initiatives.

Professional Development: Participate in events and activities aimed at advancing your personal and professional growth.

Networking Opportunities: Connect with students from diverse backgrounds and expand your horizons.

FAQs

What is TNV IIUI Chapter?

+
-

The Chapter is a sub-organ of The New Visionaries (TNV) Youth Forum, dedicated to serving students of Social Sciences. We provide a platform for students to engage in various activities focused on personal growth, leadership, and networking.

Who can join the Chapter?

+
-

The Chapter Membership is open to anyone who is passionate about personal and professional development, community engagement, and global affairs.

How can I get involved with the Chapter?

+
-

You can get involved by becoming a member and participating in our events, workshops, and volunteer opportunities. Keep an eye on our website and social media platforms for updates on upcoming activities.

What type of events does the Chapter organise?

+
-

The Chapter organises a variety of events, including Model United Nations (MUN), workshops, webinars, mock trials, academic courses, and more, aimed at fostering personal growth, community engagement, and global awareness.

How do I become a member of the Chapter?

+
-

To become a member, simply fill out the membership application form available on our Career-page. Once your application is reviewed, you will receive further instructions on how to get started.

What are the benefits of becoming a member?

+
-

As a member, you will have access to networking opportunities, professional development workshops, exclusive events, and volunteer opportunities aimed at personal and professional growth.

Is there a membership fee to join the Chapter?

+
-

Yes, the membership fee for the Chapter is Rs.500.

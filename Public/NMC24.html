Overview

Welcome to the 1st in-person National Mediation Competition (NMC’24), presented by TNV-IIUI Chapter in partnership with Musaliha International Centre for Arbitration and Dispute Resolution (MICADR). This prestigious event brings together law students, legal professionals, and mediation advocates from across Pakistan to engage in an immersive mediation competition designed to develop practical dispute resolution skills.

NMC’24 is not merely a competition; it is an initiative to establish a lasting tradition of mediation in Pakistan, encouraging young professionals to explore mediation as a critical and viable path in their legal careers. With a focus on skills development, this event will introduce participants to real-world mediation scenarios, allowing them to work as Clients, Counsels, and Mediators in a professional, high-stakes environment.

Our Vision

The idea for NMC’24 sparked one evening over a cup of tea with friends, who shared a vision to bring mediation into focus for law students. What started as a casual conversation slowly evolved into a structured plan to create a competition that would combine professional growth with prospective career opportunities.

It is Beyond Academics!

NMC'24 is designed to go beyond academics, providing students with the opportunity to consider career aspects in mediation. Through practical experience and the M-MAST Certification, participants gain critical advocacy skills, build professional connections, and enhance employability, positioning them for success as future mediation advocates, ultimately contributing to the issue of pendency of cases in Pakistan.

About MICADR’s Certified Mediation Advocacy Skills Training (M-MAST)

What is it?

MICADR’s Certified Mediation Advocacy Skills Training (M-MAST) is an extensive, high-calibre, comprehensive programme, spanning over a period of 12-16 hours, designed to equip participants with the essential skills required to excel in mediation advocacy. This course is recognised under the Islamabad High Court’s Mediation Practice Direction (Civil) Rules, 2023, and provides a practical, step-by-step guide to effective mediation advocacy.

Practical Application and Value

By completing the M-MAST training, participants will earn the prestigious title of Mediation Advocate, certified by MICADR and recognised under the Islamabad Mediation Practice Direction (Civil) Rules, 2023, by the Islamabad High Court. Meditation Advocates can skillfully represent clients in mediation, guiding them towards amicable resolutions and setting themselves apart in the legal profession.

Certification Fee Waiver for NMC’24 Participants

Typically, the M-MAST certification course is priced at PKR 20,000/- for law students. However, MICADR in collaboration with the Chapter is waiving this fee entirely for all NMC’24 registered participants. By paying a modest registration fee of PKR 3,000/- per participant, participants will gain full access to the M-MAST certification and additional, exclusive competition training sessions.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Who is eligible to participate in NMC’24?

Students currently enrolled in any HEC recognised institution across Pakistan; and Fresh Graduates having graduated no longer than six months before the competition date.

What makes NMC’24 unique compared to other legal competitions?

NMC’24 is Pakistan’s first in-person national mediation competition, blending practical mediation training with career-focused opportunities.

What is included in the registration fee?

The PKR 3,000/- per participant fee covers the M-MAST certification, exclusive pre-competition training sessions, and full event participation.

What is M-MAST, and why is it valuable?

M-MAST is a certified Mediation Advocacy Skills Training, recognised under the Islamabad Mediation Practice Direction (Civil) Rules, 2023, by the Islamabad High Court, providing participants with skills to become certified Mediation Advocates.

Will participants receive any certification?

Yes, participants who complete the M-MAST training and successfully participate in the competition will be awarded the certificate by TNV IIUI Chapter and MICADR.

What if I don’t have prior experience in mediation?

Don’t worry – we’ve got you covered. All the participating teams will receive pre-competition training sessions, along with the M-MAST certification, which will equip the teams with the essential skills needed to confidently participate the competition.

What roles will participants take on during the competition?

Participants will act as Clients, Counsels, and Mediators, simulating real-world mediation scenarios.

What are the phases of the competition?

The competition includes preliminary rounds, quarter-finals, semi-finals, and finals.

Are there any pre-competition sessions?

Yes, participants have three training sessions tentatively scheduled from December 04, 06 and 08, covering mediation skills and techniques.

How do I register for NMC’24?

Visit www.tnviiui.org or email us at nmc@tnviiui.org for the registration form and details.
